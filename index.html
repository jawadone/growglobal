FINTALK @ Bahrain
MediaOne TV is taking an initiative under the brand "Grow Global - FINTALK" to help businesses in Bahrain to best VAT preparation.

Government in Bahrain has announced introduction of VAT (Value Added Tax) in effective from 1st January 2020 at the standard rate of 5 %. The introduction of VAT would be a great challenge for the businesses in the Bahrain market. Conventionally for past many years businesses in Bahrain function largely non-tax environment. The new development is critical for small time enterprises and should be equipped to meet the critical operation procedures of the new VAT System.

VAT AWARENESS SEMINAR
The seminar in Bahrain emphasys on the empowerment of the business community to face challenges in the business environment. The seminar will be covering VAT Fundamentals, the best approach for catching up with the key aspects of changes and preparing the entrepreneurs to attain day one readiness.
Finance experts address the seminar with an interactive Q&A session to clarify all your VAT related queries.
